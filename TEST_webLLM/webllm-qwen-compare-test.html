Standalone harness for @mlc-ai/web-llm (WebGPU). Defaults use the closest official prebuilt Qwen3 q4-style weights in the current catalog: Qwen3-0.6B-q4f16_1-MLC vs Qwen3-1.7B-q4f16_1-MLC (nearest sizes to “~0.8B q4” and “~2B” until Qwen3.5 prebuilts appear). Each field is tied to a full list of prebuilt model_ids (browser autocomplete); defaults stay as below. Large models may fail to load on limited GPU RAM — errors are caught and shown without crashing the page. Not wired into the adventure game; for experimentation only.
